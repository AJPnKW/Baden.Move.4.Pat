# QA And Validation Summary

Validation is summarized at a high level in this repository.

## What Was Validated During The Real Project

- timing feasibility for move and overlap activities
- viability of renovation timing options
- practicality of mover versus self-move boundaries
- readiness dependencies for key spaces and services
- whether some ideas were true requirements versus only preferences
- whether option research justified changing scope, timing, or budget assumptions

## What Is Not Included Publicly

- raw QA notes
- private checklists
- detailed validation records
- sensitive supporting documents

## Why

The repository is meant to show project-management thinking and structure without exposing household operational details.

## Validation Approach

Validation in this project was pragmatic:

- test whether a plan could realistically fit the timing window
- test whether an option changed the scope boundary
- test whether a choice was safe to defer
- test whether partial delivery would create breakage elsewhere

That means validation was not only about quality checking after a decision. It was also part of determining what the real project scope needed to be.
