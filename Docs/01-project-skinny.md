# Project Skinny

## Problem

Coordinate a home transition project that combined sale preparation, move planning, renovation readiness, staged occupancy overlap, and household service cutovers without losing control of timing and dependencies.

## Output

A lightweight but structured move-and-transition plan with phased activities, key decisions, issue handling, and readiness checkpoints.

## Success Definition

The old home is prepared for sale activity, the new home is made ready in the right sequence, critical dates are protected, and the move is stabilized without key tasks being missed.
